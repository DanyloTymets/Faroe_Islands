Faroe Islands


Main Features

The Faroe Islands is a North Atlantic archipelago located 320 kilometres (200 mi) north-northwest of Scotland, and about halfway between Norway and Iceland.

Activities

Our archipelago suggest a huge amount of different activities such as hiking, diving, fishing and many others.
